The problem

You're using AI. Just not properly.

It usually starts the same way — a tool here, a prompt there. Before long you're spending more time fixing AI output than you would have spent just writing it yourself.

01
Tool Chaos

A different AI tool for every task. No shared system. No consistent process. Every person on the team working differently — and spending more time switching than designing.

02
Generic Outputs

Vague prompts get vague results. Without a structured approach, AI produces content that looks finished but lacks the instructional rigour that makes learning work.

Endless Rework

You spend more time editing AI output than writing from scratch. That's not an AI problem — it's a workflow problem. And it's entirely fixable.
